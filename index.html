SEO — Why is it Important?

We want people to be able to find us.

We have this great product/service/idea and people need a way to find it without having a direct link.

Web developers and marketing teams can follow a set of best practices to help people find their client in search results easily and quickly.

Website SEO Best Practices

What should you ask your web developers to do?

  • Page Title - Every page should have a unique, keyword rich title (Stream Realty Hosts Event At Trammell Crow Center)
  • Meta Description Tag - Every page should have a unique description that accurately summarizes the page's content

More SEO Best Practices

  • URL Structures - Keep them short, simple, and avoid using excessive keywords (Stream Atlanta)
  • Provide Descriptive Link Text - Avoid "click here", use something more meaningful (Meet LouLouBLUE)
  • Images - Use descriptive filenames and provide alternate text
    <img src="stream-dallas.jpg" alt="Stream Realty's Dallas Office Building" />
  • Use Proper Heading Tags - (Select Interactive Blog)
  • Create both Robots.txt and a XML site map
  • Google does factor site/page load speed (Google Page Speed)

LINKS, LINKS, LINKS!!!

The more links to your site, the better your PageRank

Google Analytics

What can we do with Google Analytics?

  • View site traffic
  • Where visitors are, what browser they use, desktop vs mobile, how they got to the site, how long they were on the site, what pages they viewed

What should we look at in the data?

  • How users get to the site? Organic searches, PPC, referrals, direct traffic
  • How long they are on the site >>> # of visitors to the site
  • Pageviews - How many pages are people visiting - Are they engaged in the site content?
  • What is the traffic flow?

Google Webmaster Tools

Why do we want to look at this?

  • The ranking of keywords Google finds in your content
  • How many external links Google has found that point to your site
  • HTML recommendations for page titles and meta descriptions
  • Alerts of missing pages or broken links
  • Pages Google has indexed
  • Googlebot crawl activity - How often Google crawls your site for content and how much content it crawls
